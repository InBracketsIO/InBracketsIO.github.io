Why us

XCard wallet is supported with the best execution engine that gathers order books from every cryptocurrency exchange in the world. That’s why we can guarantee you that the price you see is the best on the market.

XCard directly connects top 30 cryptocurrencies with any payment card, regardless of the issuing provider: Visa, Mastercard, UnionPay, JCB, Discover AMEX, Belkart - you name it - we have it.

Our loan system let us top up your payment card with fiat money using cryptocurrency as a collateral. In short term loan you will receive fiat money immediately without any additional rate of interest & you dont have to payback the loan.

Our revenue is based on spreads. You wouldn’t be with charged any additional cost for transaction.
